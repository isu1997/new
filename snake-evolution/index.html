Snake Evolution

Basic Rules

  • Start with 5 hearts
  • Pass through walls to appear on the other side
  • Avoid hitting obstacles and yourself
  • Score points by collecting food
  • Level up every 100 points

Food Types

  • Regular Food (Red) - Increases score and length
  • Heart Power-up (Orange) - Restores one heart
  • Shrink Power-up (Green) - Reduces snake length to initial size
  • Time Freeze (Blue) - Activates "Snake Platinum: The World" ability, freezing the rival snake for 5 seconds

Obstacles

  • Gray blocks appear as obstacles
  • More obstacles appear each level
  • Hitting obstacles costs one heart
  • Obstacles are randomly placed

Level 7 - Golden Snake Challenge

  • A golden rival snake appears!
  • It hunts both food and you
  • The golden snake can eat your food
  • The golden snake can only damage you when its head touches your body
  • You can defeat the golden snake by touching its tail with your head
  • Getting caught by it costs one heart
  • The golden snake grows longer when it catches you
  • Level 7 and beyond become increasingly challenging

Special Abilities

  • "Snake Platinum: The World" - Freezes the rival snake for 5 seconds while you continue to move
  • Activated by collecting the blue Time Freeze food
  • Use this strategic advantage to collect more food or target the golden snake's tail

Controls

  • Use arrow keys on keyboard
  • Use joystick on mobile devices
  • Pause button to take a break
  • Restart button to start over
